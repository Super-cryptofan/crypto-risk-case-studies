# Poly Network Hack
Category: Cross-Chain Exploit  
Year: 2021
Overview:
The Poly Network hack was one of the largest exploits in the crypto industry, involving hundreds of millions of dollars in assets.
The attacker exploited a vulnerability in the protocol's cross-chain messaging system.
## Vulnerability
The attacker was able to manipulate the cross-chain contract logic and redirect funds to their own addresses.
## Stolen Assets
The exploit initially resulted in the theft of over 600 million dollars in crypto assets.
## Unusual Outcome
The attacker later began returning the stolen funds, claiming the attack was intended to expose the vulnerability.
